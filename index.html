To set up an alert, enter your desired keywords in the textfield above and click the + NEW ALERT button. Keywords may include job title, location, job type and or skill level, for example: "tire technician", "edmonton", "alberta", "apprentice", "journeyman".

Jobs that match your alerts will be automatically displayed on this page. Matching jobs will also be displayed with an alert icon on the job list.

You can also sign up to receive daily notifications by email or RSS when matching jobs are posted.

Important: The alert system identifies you as the user through a unique ID that is saved in your browser through a cookie. Deleting cookies from xtrades.ca will cause your alerts to be deleted.
